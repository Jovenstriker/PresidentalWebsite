About Henry

Henry is hardworking, sensible, and honest. Coming from nothing, he knows what pain and determination is; what the everyday folk have to endure without reward.

He grew up in the rural, crime-ridden town of Livingston, Alabama. His parents did not have college degrees; and they worked two shifts at the local Sal's burger shack. His brother and mother were killed by a drive by from the Mexican Cartel after they stood up against extortion.

His family's strong integrity and determination greatly influenced him. He swore that he will create a better America. Henry persevered and went on to get his doctorate in politics and philosophy, and a masters in economics Now, Henry has a plan. He has a plan to re-assemble this country, to unite the states, and to restore our cities
