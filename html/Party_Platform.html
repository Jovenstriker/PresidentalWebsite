American Priorities

American is not in the best shape right now. But Henry has a plan to help us out.

  • Limited Government on the Economy

    America is a place where people can spend their passion and dreams to make a living.
    Coupled with the rising inflation and oncoming recession, we don't need the government
    strangling the American Dream with strict regulation and limitations on the people.

  • Reduced Taxes

    Instead of limiting the economy by strict regulation, the government should instead support and reduce taxes for businesses that fuel the economy.
    Individual achievement leads to individual success.

    Reducing taxes also helps everyone in America, the economy, the country, will prosper and the tradeoff will be worth for everyone involved

  • Gun Control

    Last year there were 694,050 violent crimes in the US, a 28.64% increase from last year!

    Henry believes that everyone should be allowed their constitutional right to bear arms and safety.

  • Streamline Immigration Process

    America is built on diversity and dreams. We warmly welcome legal immigrants but illegal immigration will be turned away.
    We need to streamline our immigration process so that it is easier to gain legal citizenship.

    Our immigration policy is not a process of prejudice or racism, but a process of safety.

  • Current Priorities Trump Environmental Regulations

    We need to focus on the dozens of present problems facing America right now: an economic recession, Covid-19, poverty.

    Environmental regulations will be a burden on the already straining economy.
    When we solve our problems, we can then tackle global warming together. Helping the present will help the future.

  • Main Point

    Henry's main point is to help the people protect and build their livelihoods for themselves, just like how he struggled to do. He believes individual independence and self-sufficiency will create a better, stronger America.
